The Hot Shots is a project as well as a dance group. We produce full length shows for theatre settings with a historic educational point of view but does also specializes in one-time performances at all kinds of festivities. Many years of experience in the industry make us uncommonly flexible when it comes to designing the size and scope of our involvement to suit your needs.
